Commission & Pricing

Makerly keeps pricing simple and transparent: a flat 5% commission on completed projects, with clear fees for both clients and freelancers.

Simple flat 5% model

Many freelance marketplaces use complex tiers or charge both sides hidden fees. Makerly keeps it easy to understand.

  • For freelancers: 5% commission on the project amount you earn.
  • For clients: Transparent service fee shown at checkout.
  • No hidden charges: Dual currency view (PKR and USD) so everyone sees the value clearly.
  • Local focus: JazzCash, Easypaisa and bank transfer support planned as core payment channels.

This model is designed to be cheaper than typical 20%+ fees on global platforms while still making the platform sustainable as it grows.[web:120][web:123]
